About Us

In alignment with India's vision to become a global leader in Science and Technology and to foster unprecedented economic growth, the Government of India identified the need for enhanced technical manpower. To address this, the decision was made to establish several new institutions focused on technology and management, including Centurion University of Technology & Management, Bhubaneswar (CUTM).

Founded in 2005, CUTM is dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation. The university offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines, including engineering, management, and applied sciences. CUTM is known for its emphasis on practical learning and industry collaboration, preparing students for the challenges of the global job market.

The university operates from a sprawling campus that features modern infrastructure, including state-of-the-art laboratories, a well-equipped library, and dedicated spaces for research and innovation. CUTM encourages an interdisciplinary approach, allowing students to gain insights across various fields of study.

The Academic Office at CUTM is responsible for managing all academic affairs and ensuring that students receive the necessary support throughout their educational journey. The office comprises a Dean of Academic Affairs, Associate Deans, and administrative staff dedicated to fostering a conducive learning environment.

With its commitment to excellence and innovation, Centurion University of Technology & Management, Bhubaneswar, aims to contribute significantly to the development of skilled professionals equipped to meet the demands of the industry and society.
